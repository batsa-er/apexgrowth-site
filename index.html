The Diagnosis

Your marketing isn't broken.
Your revenue system is.

Most enterprise and high-growth businesses in Africa aren't struggling because of bad products or poor teams. They're struggling because their marketing operates as a cost centre — disconnected from revenue, automation, and data — instead of as a growth engine.

"If your CMO can't tell you exactly how many qualified opportunities marketing generated last quarter, what it cost per pipeline dollar, and what the compounding trajectory looks like — you don't have a marketing department. You have an expense."
— Apex Growth Diagnostic Framework

01

Inconsistent Lead Flow

Revenue depends on the sales team's personal network and referrals. There is no systematic, scalable demand generation engine producing qualified pipeline month after month.

02

Zero Marketing ROI Visibility

Spend goes out on campaigns but no one can say with confidence what it returned. Attribution is guesswork. Decisions are emotional, not data-driven — which means the right investments don't get made.

03

Sales & Marketing Misalignment

Marketing generates noise. Sales ignores it. There's no shared definition of a qualified lead, no SLA, no feedback loop — just two teams pulling in opposite directions while revenue suffers.

04

No Automation or CRM Infrastructure

Leads fall through the cracks. Follow-up is manual and inconsistent. Customer data lives in spreadsheets. The business cannot scale its commercial operation without hiring 10 more salespeople.
